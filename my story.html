How It All Started

My name is Sylvia Nyaga, and I've always been fascinated by how creativity and technology come together to build beautiful things. Growing up, I was the kind of person who loved experimenting. Whether it was sketching designs, customizing my phone or helping friends fix small tech issues. I never realized it then, but those small curosities were the first step into the world of web develoment.

When i wrote the first few lines of HTML, I felt like I'd unlocked a secret language that could bring my ideas to life. Over time, I started exploring CSS to make pages more stylish and later Javascript to make them interactive. What started as a hobby slowly turned into a passion and that passion has shaped who I am today.

Beyond coding, I'm deeply ispired by storytelling. I believe every website tells a story of a brand, a dream or a person and my goal is to use design and development to tell this stories beautifully. I'm currently working on growing my skills to become a full-stack web developer and one day create digital experiences that make people smile, think and connect.

This journey has taught me patience,problem-solving and the importance of always staying curious. Each line of code I write is a reminder that growth happens step by step and I'm just getting started.
